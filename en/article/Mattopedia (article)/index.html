Mattopedia is a Wikipedia clone, designed to run on 99% of internet-supporting devices.

Fun facts:

  • Its programming languages are HTML, CSS & JS.
  • It is developed by one developer!
  • It was made to teach people on old and new devices.
  • The site had so-called "Articles Of The Day".

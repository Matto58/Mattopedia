Water

Article in Mattopedia

Creator's note: Tempature unit is Celsius

Water (H2O/Dihydrogen monoxide/2Hydrogen 1Oxygen) is a liquid in room tempature (~21 degrees).
Its freezing point is 0 degrees, and starts vaporizing at ~60 degrees, but its vaporizing point is actually 100 degrees.
